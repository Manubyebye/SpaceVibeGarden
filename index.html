About Us

At Cosmic Grow, we are dedicated to the legal and responsible cultivation of medicinal and recreational cannabis in the Czech Republic. Our passion for botany and meticulous plant care allows us to offer products of the highest quality.

We document every stage of the cultivation process, from seed germination to final harvest, ensuring transparency and education about the complete process.

Our Strains

Varieties we cultivate with dedication

Red Hot Cookies

Typ: 40% Indica 60% Sativa

Red Hot Cookies is a slightly sativa dominant hybrid strain (60% sativa/40% indica) created through crossing the delicious Tropicanna Cookies X Tangie strains.

80-120cm High yield

Apple Fritter

hybrid 50% indica / 50% sativa

THC: 22% - 28%

Apple Fritter is a rare evenly balanced hybrid strain (50% indica/50% sativa) created through crossing the classic Sour Apple X Animal Cookies strains.

100-150cm Medium-high yield

Cali Cake

Indica Dominant Hybrid - 80% Indica / 20% Sativa

THC: 26% - 34%

Cali Cake is an indica dominant hybrid strain created through crossing the iconic Gorilla Glue #4 X LA Kush strains. Perfect for true indica lovers seeking great potency.

100-150cm High yield
